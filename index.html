🚫 Draft Action Plan — The Boycott

We propose a boycott of the next DPS unit.

Reasons for targeting a DPS rather than a support:

  • Gives us time to get as many clans and players on board as possible — Truffle's release is too soon to organise mass collective action.
  • Easier to persuade players to boycott a DPS than a support unit.
  • Most players will eventually obtain Truffle; boycotting its event means players pay more for it, which would increase dev profit — the opposite of our goal.

Leading up to the boycott, we will submit a joint statement to the developers signed by over 100 clans, outlining our concerns and notifying them of our planned actions. This will carry a weight the devs have never seen before from the community.

On a designated date, all participating clans will ask their players to remove all decorations from their player profiles as a show of solidarity — a visible signal to devs of the strength of feeling across the entire player base.

Further actions under discussion include non-participation in Round Table events and playtests.

📋 Open Discussion Points
2–3 Coordinators needed to take the Action Plan to completion — ideally the most experienced long-time players among us. See the separate Coordination & Planning thread.
Target unit consensus: Do we have agreement that the boycott targets the next DPS? Vote in the designated channel.
Player profile changes (proposed by Pample): When do they go live? Exactly what changes? Does it include a clan description banner? How long do they stay in effect? (Proposals range from a few days to the full run-up to the boycott.)
